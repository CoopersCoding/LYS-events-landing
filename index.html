Banff Day Trip

Spend the day exploring the incredible scenery of the Canadian Rockies during this day trip from Calgary to the mountain town of Banff and its surrounding attractions. Check out the surreal rock formations near Tunnel Mountain Drive, shaped by thousands of years of wind and rain. Then soar above Banff on board the Banff Gondola as you take in incredible views of the town and surrounding peaks. At the conclusion of the tour, you have the option of being dropped off in Banff for your own leisure before your shuttle departs back to Calgary.

Canmore

Encircled by the snow-capped peaks of the Canadian Rockies and just minutes from the eastern entrance of Banff National Park, this authentic mining town has long had a reputation among winter-sports aficionados, especially those headed to the cross-country skiing trails at Canmore Nordic Centre Provincial Park. Today, Canmore has become a year-round destination because of warm-weather activities like hiking, mountain biking, and whitewater rafting. Straddling the banks of the Bow River, Canmore has a strollable downtown filled with boutiques, galleries, and Canadaâ€™s dreamiest bookstore. There are three breweries, two distilleries, and an eclectic food scene that attracts dining from all around Alberta.
